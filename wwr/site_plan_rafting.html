Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to help reach more customers to use our white water rafting services. We will advertise the various different equipment that they can buy or rent. We will also advertise our expert white water rafting guide services. We will advertise our family owned, family operated, and family friendly brand. We will provide them with the information that they need to find our stores and learn more about our company. We will also feature all of our white water rafting product sales going on at our stores, and all of our fantastic professional rafting guides. Additionally, we will inform them on the quality of our products, and why to choose our products and our guides for their rafting trips. Finally, we will provide them with a booking option on the website, so they can book their guide and equipment in advance of their trip without having to call or visit our store.

Audience

Our target audience will be people who love the great outdoors, but don't have the experience to go rafting on their own. The majority of our audience will be families with parents and young children as well as other outdoors youth groups. These families and youth groups will need to be in a good middle to upper class financial situation to afford to go out on these rafting trips, and to buy or rent rafting products, and to use rafting guides for their trip. They will want to have the best, most fun rafting experience they can get for the money that they're spending. They will want reliable rafting products, and fun professional guides that will help them have a fun experience without worrying about the danger that can come with it. Our audience will most likely be accessing this website by computer or laptop, since they will be planning their trips well in advance, and will want to schedule and pay everything while they're scheduling their trip.
